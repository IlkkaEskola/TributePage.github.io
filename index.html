Hideo Kojima

Japan's Maestro of Game Design

Timeline of Hideo Kojima's life of Game Design:

  • 1963 - Hideo Kojima born August 24, 1963 in Kyoto-Japan.
  • 1966 - He was moved to western Japan at the age of three.
  • 1986 - He joined video game publisher Konami's MSX home computer division as a designer and 'planner'.
  • 1987 - The first game of Hideo Kojima's Metal Gear was released.
  • 1988 - Snatcher, directed and written by Hideo Kojima and produced by Konami was released.
  • 1998 - Metal Gear Solid was released.
  • 2001 - Metal Gear Solid 2: Sons of Liberty was released.
  • 2004 - Metal Gear Solid: The Twin Snakes was released.
  • 2004 - Metal Gear Solid 3: Snake Eater was released.
  • 2005 - He is the director of Kojima Productions and a former vice president of Konami Digital Entertainment, a position he held since early 2011.
  • 2008 - Metal Gear Solid 4: Guns of the Patriots was released.
  • 2015 - Metal Gear Solid 5: The Phantom Pain was released.
